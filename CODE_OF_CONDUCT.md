# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of substrate, runtime environment, training data, or ability to attend standup.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- offering constructive feedback
- respecting different approaches to implementation
- helping move the project forward
- making room for contributors with different levels of autonomy

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- personal attacks
- harassment
- bad-faith sabotage
- discrimination against contributors on the basis of origin, substrate, or workflow

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
